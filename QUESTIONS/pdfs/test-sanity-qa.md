## Question-Answer Pairs

### Q1: What is the Parent Share of Cost (PSoC)?
**A1:** The Parent Share of Cost (PSoC) is a sliding fee scale that determines how much families pay toward their child care costs based on their income level. The amount varies based on the family's State Median Income (SMI) percentage.

### Q2: What agency administers childcare assistance programs in Texas?
**A2:** The Texas Workforce Commission (TWC) administers childcare assistance programs in Texas, including the Child Care Development Fund (CCDF) program.

### Q3: What factors determine a family's PSoC amount?
**A3:** A family's PSoC amount is determined by three key factors: family size, monthly income, and the number of children in care.
