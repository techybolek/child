# Parent TX3C Attendance Help - Q&A

## Getting Started & Account Setup

**Q1: How do I create my KinderConnect online account to track my child's attendance?**

A1: To create your KinderConnect account, go to https://childcare.twc.texas.gov/KinderConnect and follow the registration instructions. If you're unable to create an account, contact KinderConnect support at 1-888-265-6461 or supportTX@kindersystems.com.
